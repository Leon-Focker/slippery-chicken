Events

There will be a series of workshops, concerts, and paper presentations on slippery chicken during 2012.

Workshops

Two free three-day workshops introducing slippery chicken will focus on getting new users up and running with the system and the practical applications of its algorithms. We intend to run these over the summer of 2012, once in Edinburgh (in English) and once at ZKM Karlsruhe (in German and English).


Further workshops will take place in 2013 at the Folkwang Hochschule, Essen, Germany, 16-18 May, 2013 (to be confirmed).


See the workshops page for more details.
